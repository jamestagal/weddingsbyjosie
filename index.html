Welcome To Weddings By Josie

"Tie the knot with Josie"

It will be my pleasure to help you bring to reality your dreams for a beautiful ceremony. This can be as short, as long, as quirky, as different, as formal/informal as you want it to be. I only do ONE ceremony a day so my whole focus will be on you, on the day.

Certified Celebrant

Unique Ceremony

My Service

Meet Face-To-Face

We have an inital chat about my service with no obligation to book me.

Book an appointment

You make a booking with me to lock in the date.

Meet up

We meet up at your convenience to complete the legal documents.

Have a rehearsal

I attend the rehearsal and run through the procedings so everything goes smoothly on the day.

Get you married!

I marry you according to your wishes on your big day.

After the ceremony

I lodge the paperwork to in the state or territory where the ceremony is performed.
