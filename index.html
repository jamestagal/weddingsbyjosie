We Provide The Best Service

As a Canberra Marriage Celebrant I conduct, marriage ceremonies, same sex couples and also Naming Day and Renewal of Vows Ceremonies.

Meet Face-To-Face

We have an inital chat about my service with not obligations.

Book an appointment

You make a booking with me to lock in the ceremony.

Meet up

We meet up to complete the legals at a minimum of 1 month before the ceremony.

Have a rehearsal

Get you married!

I marry you according to your wishes on your big day.
